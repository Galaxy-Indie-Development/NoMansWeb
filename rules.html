No Man's MC Lawbook


General Rules
  • No Sexual Jokes, Sexual Talk, Racist Talk, Racist Jokes or anything of the sorts!
  • English only in General Chat!
  • Be respectful to all staff members!
  • Destruction of anything unclaimed 5 chunks from spawn's borders is immediately perma bannable
  • No killing players in any plots labeled Safezone, Failure to follow this will result in a immediate ban!
Town Rules
  • All town names must be appropriate and semi realistic. You will get one warning then the offending town will be removed!
  • No Snake Claims! Any claim that is part of a snake claim will be removed without comepensation!
  • Insiding is allowed as long as it doesn't involve griefing or theft!
  • Towns must be atleast 3 chunks from another town unless said town is within a nation with the other town!
  • Towns must be atleast a minimum of 20 chunks from spawn! They will be removed otherwise!
